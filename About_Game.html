GAME DESCRIPTION

The Joker jumps across the river while cards appear on the screen. Your task is to choose the correct card before it disappears.

But be careful! Sometimes the Dark Joker appears — a dangerous enemy that must be avoided at all costs!

With every level, the game becomes faster and more difficult, requiring maximum concentration and quick reactions.

Set records, unlock new skins, and become the ultimate Joker!
